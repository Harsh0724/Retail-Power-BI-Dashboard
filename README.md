🔹 Project Title

Retail Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

🔹 📌 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance across different regions, categories, and customer segments. It enables tracking of key business metrics such as sales, profit, and order trends to support data-driven decision-making.

🔹 📁 Dataset
Dataset: Retail / Maven Market Dataset
Size: ~2,68,000+ records (combined datasets)
Features: Sales, Profit, Orders, Products, Customers, Regions, Shipping
🔹 📊 Key Insights
Technology category emerged as the most profitable segment, driven by high-margin products
Certain categories showed high sales but low or negative profit, indicating inefficiencies
Sales performance varied significantly across regions, highlighting geographic opportunities
Customer segments contributed differently to revenue, with key segments driving majority sales
Identified trends in sales growth and seasonal variations across time periods
🔹 🛠 Tools & Technologies
Power BI
Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
DAX (Measures & KPIs)
🔹 📈 Dashboard Features
KPI cards for Sales, Profit, and Orders
Interactive filters (Region, Category, Segment)
Category-wise and region-wise performance analysis
Trend analysis for sales and profitability
🔹 💼 Business Value

This dashboard helps businesses:

Identify profitable and underperforming areas
Optimize product and regional strategies
Understand customer contribution to revenue
Make informed, data-driven decisions
